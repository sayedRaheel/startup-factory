### The Market Pain Point
Developers today are constantly burdened by heavy local environments. Running full stacks of microservices via Docker Compose consumes massive CPU, drains battery life on modern laptops, and slows down the feedback loop. 

### The Gap
There is a massive gap for a local-first tool that can "downsample" an entire architecture—swapping out heavy PostgreSQL or Elasticsearch containers with file-based equivalents like SQLite and DuckDB just for local development.

### The Opportunity
A CLI that instantly "nano-fies" heavy environments would save thousands of hours of compile and spin-up time.
